Vission

The vision of the Department of Information and Communication Engineering (ICE) is to be a centre of excellence and a world-class institution of higher learning and innovation through research in ICE fields and facilitate enrichment of ICE human resources- the faculty, student, and staff, thereby establishing itself as a center of excellence at the national and international level.

Mission

Students and Faculties of ICE, NSTU are constantly accomplishing prospective and technical activities all through the year. One of these is Programming Contests. The ICE Department has organized a programming club for the following purposes:

  • To provide world-class quality education to graduates in ICE and allied fields and train the students to effectively apply this education to solve real-world problems, thereby contributing towards the benefit of our country and the humanity.
  • To nurture the students through quality teaching, research and innovation in ICE fields.
  • To enhance the strength of the faculty members at all levels.
  • To guide the students develop themselves as human beings with high ethical and moral values.
  • To provide advanced expertise in the information and communication technologies; including Information Systems and Technology, Computer Science and Software Engineering, Electrical, Computer and Telecommunications Engineering.
  • To enrich our student's potential, make them a life-long learner and finally equip and prepare them to face the complex challenges of the 21st century and beyond.
  • To disseminate the knowledge gained from the researches to the stakeholders through scholarly publications.
