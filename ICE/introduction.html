Introduction about ICE

The department of Information and Communication Engineering is a promising and leading department in NSTU under the faculty of Engineering and Technology. The dept. is accomplishing numerous activities to become an extraordinary one in terms of academic and technical infrastructure not only in Bangladesh but also worldwide. Since its establishment in 2012, it is well-recognized for providing quality education to students by a group of committed teachers, excellent management, work culture and enormous facilities. It has three class rooms, each equipped with multimedia projectors, programming and electronics labs along with internet facilities, seminar libraries equipped with innumerable books, journals, and research documents. It also has an active programming club. ICE dept. has now five batches running; 269 students in total. The dept. is guided by a dedicated and hardworking chairman Engg. Professor Dr. Mohammad Ashikur Rahman Khan sir. ICE can play the most important role in the development of economy of developing countries. There are a lot of scopes of setting up more andmore software industries in our country. The department has ambitious plans to become a role model in Bangladesh and in the region. It possesses the basic ingredients for achieving this goal in terms of the qualifications of its department members and staff, and in terms of its IT infrastructure. The department of ICE is committed to maintaining the outstanding quality of its academic programs, research, faculty and graduates.
